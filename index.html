Covering the best, most entertaining, and most popular racing leagues around the world

Lights out and away we go!

Formula 1 (F1)

Formula 1 is considered the pinnacle of motorsport, as it is a racing competition between the fastest cars made. What makes this sport even more intriguing is that it is also a sport that focuses on engineering the best possible car. It is not only an insane racing sport, but it is also like a big science fair project for the teams.

  • Most Driver World Championships: Tie between Michael Schumacher and Lewis Hamilton (7)
  • Most Constructor World Championships: Scuderia Ferrari
  • Most Popular Race: Monaco Grand Prix

NASCAR

NASCAR is probably one of the most popular form of motorsport in the US after F1. It is a form of stock car racing where drivers will be placed in their cars and race for 3-4 hours around different tracks like ovals and road courses. The movie "Cars" is also based on this sport, and it features multiple drivers like Dale Earnhardt Jr, Richard Petty, and Jeff Gordon.

  • Most Driver Championships: Tie between Richard Petty, Dale Earnhardt Sr, and Jimmie Johnson (7)
  • Most Successful Team: Hendrick Motorsports
  • Most Popular Race: Daytona 500

MotoGP

MotoGP stands for Grand Prix motorcyle racing and is the highest class of racing for motorcycles. All motorsports are dangerous, but this features riders not having any protection if they crash. They have to wear a lot of protective gear because crashes are very frequent in this sport. The riders also get very low on their bikes to make very sharp turns.

  • Most Driver World Championships: Giacomo Agostini (15)
  • Most Successful Team: Repsol Honda Team
  • Most Popular Race: TT Assen

IndyCar

IndyCar can be described as a mix of Formula 1 and NASCAR, as they are an open wheel series that have road course races, and they race on ovals. They also share a lot of the tracks that are used in the US, like Circuit of the Americas, and Indianapolis Speedway. The cars are extremely quick as they can reach up to speeds of 240 miles per hour. The movie "Turbo" is also based on this racing series.

  • Most Driver Championships: AJ Foyt (7)
  • Most Successful Teams (The Big 3): Ganassi, Andretti, and Penske
  • Most Popular Race: Indy 500

World Endurance Championship (WEC)

The World Endurance Championship is the premier racing series for sports cars. This series has multiple classes including the Hypercar class, LMP2 (will not feature on the grid anymore), and Supercars. Multiple famous brands compete in this series like Audi, Porsche, and more. These races feature extremely long races where drivers will have to take shifts as they can stretch to as long as 24 hours.

  • Most Driver World Championships : Sébastien Buemi and Brendon Hartley (4)
  • Most Successful Team: Audi and Porsche
  • Most Popular Race: 24 Hours at Le Mans

World Rally Championship (WRC)

The World Rally Championship is a racing competition like no other. It features many off road tracks that drivers have to navigate through, and they must do it in a fast time. It is basically a battle against elements and the clock. This is easily one of the toughest forms of racing with no regular set race tracks, but instead they are driving on snow, dirt, and up mountains.

  • Most Driver Championships: Sébastien Loeb (9)
  • Most Successful Teams: Citroën and Toyota
  • Most Popular Race: Safari Rally
